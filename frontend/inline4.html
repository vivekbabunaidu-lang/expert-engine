Intake Manifold

Internal Diagnostics
The cast-aluminum structure running the full length of the head.
System Function

[ 02 / 10 ] SYS-I4-FL

Fuel Rail & Injectors

Internal Diagnostics
Parallel slender pipe with 4 sequential injectors plugging into ports.
System Function
Supplies a fine mist of petrol into the port before Intake Valve ignition.
[ 03 / 10 ] SYS-I4-HD

Cylinder Head

Internal Diagnostics
A single long rectangular casting bolted across the block top.
System Function
Houses the entire Valvetrain and acts as the combustion roof.
[ 04 / 10 ] SYS-I4-CS

Camshaft (DOHC)

Internal Diagnostics
Two long shafts with egg-shaped lobes running side by side.
System Function
Pushes valves open at the exact point in the 4-Stroke Cycle.
[ 05 / 10 ] SYS-I4-IGN

Spark Plugs

Internal Diagnostics
4 plugs threaded vertically into the cylinder head.
System Function
Fires a 20kV+ spark at the peak of Compression to ignite the charge.
[ 06 / 10 ] SYS-I4-BLK

Cylinder Block

Internal Diagnostics
A compact, upright rectangular block with 4 vertical cylinder bores.
System Function
The engine's primary backbone; contains Oil Galleries and water jackets.
[ 07 / 10 ] SYS-I4-PST

Pistons & Rods

Internal Diagnostics
4 cylindrical pistons firing in the specific 1-3-4-2 sequence.
System Function
Convert vertical pressure into linear motion via Connecting Rods.
[ 08 / 10 ] SYS-I4-CRK

Crankshaft

Internal Diagnostics
A single straight crankshaft with 4 offset pins at 180° intervals.
System Function
Converts the combined piston motion into high-velocity Rotation.
[ 09 / 10 ] SYS-I4-TIB

Timing Belt / Chain

Internal Diagnostics
High-tensile link connecting the crankshaft to the dual cams.
System Function
Maintains the critical 2:1 Ratio to prevent piston-valve collision.
[ 10 / 10 ] SYS-I4-SUM

Oil Pan & Sump

Internal Diagnostics
The stamped-steel pan forming the engine's structural base.
System Function
The primary oil reservoir block; essential for Vibration Damping.
