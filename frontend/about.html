FUNDAMENTALS // 4-STROKE ENGINE

THE MECHANICAL SYMPHONY

A precision dance of air, fuel, and fire. Scroll to analyze the cycle.

01

INTAKE

The piston moves down, creating a vacuum. The intake valve opens, pulling in a crisp air-fuel mixture ready for the punch.

02

COMPRESSION

Valves close tightly. The piston shoots upward, crushing the mixture into a tiny, high-pressure, highly explosive pocket.

03

POWER

💥 BOOM. The spark plug fires. The resulting explosion drives the piston down with thousands of pounds of force.

04

EXHAUST

The job is done. The exhaust valve opens, and the piston pushes the spent gases out to make room for the next cycle.
